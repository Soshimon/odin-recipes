Pizza

Photo by Valerio Capello licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported license.

Cheesy, saucy, and endlessly customizable.

Pizza is an Italian classic loved around the world, made from a simple yeasted dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and endless topping combinations. Baked in a hot oven, it develops a golden, bubbly crust with melted cheese on top. Depending on how you make it, the crust can be soft and chewy or crisp and crunchy, giving each bite a perfect mix of texture and flavor.

Ingredients

For the dough:
  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water (about 110°F / 45°C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
For the sauce:
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the toppings:
  • 250g fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

For the dough:
  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let stand for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a rough dough forms.
  3. Transfer to a lightly floured surface and knead for 8-10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp towel, and let rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, until doubled in size.
For the sauce
  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened. Remove from heat and let cool.
Assemble and bake
  1. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Punch down risen dough and divide into 2-3 portions (depending on desired pizza size). Roll out on a floured surface to about ¼ inch thickness.
  3. Transfer dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet (or onto a pizza peel if using a stone).
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough. Add torn mozzarella pieces evenly across the surface.
  5. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove pizza from oven and top with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
